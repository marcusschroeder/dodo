Please read, before you start

The first step is to feed DODO with an olfactory matrix. It prefers well formatted *.csv files either separated with either semicolons or commas. This data-chunk should not be larger than 2 MB.

A little information about the structure of your *.csv file. Make sure, your file provides information about receptors and odorants, e.g. the first row should contain the names of the olfactory receptors and the first column should contain the names of the odorants. If the opposite is true, that is fine. Just make sure you do not have measurement information in the first row or first column.
